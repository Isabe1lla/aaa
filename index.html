Setting

Hailsham School: A boarding school where Kathy, Ruth, and Tommy grow up. It appears idyllic but hides dark truths about their future as organ donors.

  • Hailsham fosters creativity and encourages artwork submission for "The Gallery."
  • The school is isolated, creating a sense of both safety and foreboding.
